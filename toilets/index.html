How Sat Lav works

Sat Lav focuses on making public toilet information easy to use, especially when you need it quickly. It shows nearby locations, highlights what is known, and avoids guessing when information is incomplete.

  • Shows nearby public toilets on a live map
  • Displays walking distance and estimated time
  • Highlights accessibility, baby-changing, and radar key information when known
  • Shows when a location was last verified
