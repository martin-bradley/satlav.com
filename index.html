Designed to be simple

Sat Lav doesn’t require an account, doesn’t track you, and doesn’t try to do too much. It’s designed to be opened, used, and closed — without fuss.

What Sat Lav does

  • Shows nearby public toilets on a live map
  • Displays walking distance and estimated time
  • Highlights accessibility, baby-changing, and radar key information when known
  • Community ratings for cleanliness and safety
  • “Last verified” status so you know how fresh the information is
  • Add or update toilets to help keep the map accurate

Data & coverage

Sat Lav includes public toilet data from UK local authorities and open datasets. Where information is unknown, it’s shown clearly as unknown — nothing is guessed.
