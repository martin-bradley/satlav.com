Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2025

Sat Lav does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. The app uses your device’s location solely to show nearby public toilets and to improve the accuracy of search results. Location data is processed on your device and not stored on our servers.

We use Google Firebase Firestore to retrieve and update toilet information (such as name, ratings, and accessibility features). No user accounts are required to use the app, and we do not collect or link any personal data to your usage.

If you have any questions, please contact us at you@example.com.
