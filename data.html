Data & Coverage

Sat Lav includes public toilet information sourced from UK local authorities and open data sources. Coverage continues to improve over time, with a focus on accuracy and accessibility information.

Unknown information

Where information such as accessibility, baby changing, radar key requirement, opening hours, or charges is unknown, it is shown clearly as unknown. Sat Lav does not infer or guess missing information.
